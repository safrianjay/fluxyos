Frequently asked questions

Short answers for dropshipping owners, paid ads operators, and finance admins.

How does FluxyOS identify poor ad spend?

FluxyOS connects ad spend to orders, supplier cost, shipping, fees, COD returns, and payouts so campaigns that fail to create net margin are flagged early.

Can I track profit by product or campaign?

Yes. Revenue, ad spend, supplier bills, courier costs, payment fees, and returns can be grouped by product, campaign, or channel.

Does FluxyOS work for COD businesses?

Yes. It helps track COD returns, courier fees, pending payouts, supplier deposits, and ad top-ups in one cash view.

Can I use it without a finance team?

Yes. Owners, media buyers, and admins can read the numbers without building complicated spreadsheets.

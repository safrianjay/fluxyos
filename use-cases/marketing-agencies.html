Frequently asked questions

Short answers to what agency owners and operators usually ask before signing up.

Can FluxyOS separate client media spend from agency revenue?

Yes. FluxyOS tags pass-through ad spend separately from service revenue, then assigns it to the right client, campaign, or budget.

Can I see profitability by client?

Yes. Each client view can include retainers, project invoices, contractor cost, creative production, software, reimbursements, and media spend.

How does FluxyOS handle PPN and client billing?

FluxyOS keeps a PPN-ready audit trail for client invoices, vendor bills, reimbursements, and pass-through expenses.

What if we manage both retainers and one-off projects?

FluxyOS handles both. Retainers, campaign fees, project deposits, production budgets, and reimbursements stay separated by client and job.

Do I need a finance team to set this up?

No. FluxyOS starts with agency-friendly categories for retainers, media spend, freelancers, production, software, reimbursements, and tax.

Is there a free tier for small agencies?

Yes. FluxyOS has a free tier for smaller teams. Paid plans unlock more client accounts, AI Agents, approval flows, and advanced profitability reporting.

Frequently asked questions

Short answers for founders, CEOs, and owner-operators.

Is FluxyOS for founders without a finance team?

Yes. Founder-led teams can use FluxyOS to understand revenue, OpEx, margin, bills, subscriptions, and records that need attention before building a larger finance function.

Can FluxyOS show whether my business is doing well?

FluxyOS shows operating finance signals such as revenue, expenses, gross margin, payables, subscriptions, vendor spend, and needs-action records so founders can assess business health earlier.

Can FluxyOS help me understand cash pressure?

Yes. FluxyOS can show cash pressure proxies from bills, subscriptions, pending payables, and expected revenue. Full runway accuracy depends on available data, including real bank balance and expected collections.

Can FluxyOS replace my accountant?

No. FluxyOS is a finance operating layer before accounting and reporting. It helps organize records and explain business activity, while accountants still handle formal accounting, tax, and compliance work.

Can Fluxy AI answer founder-level finance questions?

Yes. Fluxy AI answers from FluxyOS finance data, explains backend-calculated numbers, and returns clear no-data answers when records are not available.

Does FluxyOS move money or approve payments automatically?

No. FluxyOS helps review, track, and organize finance records. Sensitive actions such as saving, approving, deleting, or marking bills paid require explicit confirmation.

Can I use FluxyOS before month-end reporting?

Yes. The page is designed around earlier visibility, so founders can see what changed, what is risky, and what needs attention before month-end reports are finalized.

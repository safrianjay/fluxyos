Frequently asked questions

How does FluxyOS help retail and franchise owners?

FluxyOS brings POS revenue, supplier invoices, outlet expenses, receipts, budgets, vendor payments, and bank settlements into one finance operations view so owners can compare true branch profitability.

Can FluxyOS compare outlet profitability?

Yes. Each transaction can be tagged by outlet so owners and finance teams can compare revenue, operating cost, supplier spend, budget variance, and net margin by branch.

Does FluxyOS track footfall or CCTV analytics?

No. FluxyOS is focused on finance operations: POS revenue, settlement, supplier costs, store expenses, receipts, budgets, reconciliation, and branch-level P&L.

Can store managers submit receipts from WhatsApp?

Yes. Store teams can send receipts through WhatsApp, email, or upload. FluxyOS extracts vendor, amount, tax, and category for finance review.

Frequently asked questions

Dynamic Budgeting questions, answered

What is Dynamic Budgeting?

+

Dynamic Budgeting is a FluxyOS feature that lets businesses allocate, track, and adjust budgets in real time as spend changes. Instead of waiting for spreadsheet updates, teams can see available budget, burn rate, and variance as transactions arrive.

How does Dynamic Budgeting help Indonesian SMBs?

+

Dynamic Budgeting helps Indonesian SMBs control spending by connecting budgets to live vendor, receipt, and ledger data. Owners can see when marketing, operations, SaaS, or project budgets are moving off plan before month end.

Can FluxyOS track budgets by department or project?

+

Yes. FluxyOS can organize budgets by department, project, entity, or operating category so teams can compare planned spend against actual spend. This is useful for agencies, e-commerce teams, restaurants, and multi-location operators.

Does Dynamic Budgeting send alerts?

+

Yes. Dynamic Budgeting can surface variance and burn-rate alerts when a budget is being used faster than expected. The goal is to help teams reallocate funds or pause spend before a budget problem becomes urgent.

Can Dynamic Budgeting work with multi-currency spend?

+

Yes. Dynamic Budgeting is designed for IDR-first reporting while supporting multi-currency spend across vendors, cards, and international platforms. This helps teams manage local budgets even when some tools bill in USD or SGD.

Is Dynamic Budgeting different from a spreadsheet budget?

+

Yes. A spreadsheet budget is usually static and updated manually, while Dynamic Budgeting updates from live finance operations data. That makes it easier to monitor runway, category spend, and budget variance without rebuilding reports each week.
